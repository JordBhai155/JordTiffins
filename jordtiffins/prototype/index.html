PRESENTING

JordFins

JordFins, the brainchild of the dynamic duo Mr. Jayant Kumar Gautam and Mr. Gyanchand Gautam, is not just a company; it's a harbinger of innovation in the realm of everyday products. One of their standout offerings is a revolutionary smart tiffin that ingeniously keeps your food warm with the convenience of a rechargeable battery. This innovative approach to food preservation has positioned JordFins as a trailblazer in the market, redefining the way we experience daily meals. What sets JordFins apart is not just its cutting-edge technology but also its unwavering commitment to safety. The smart tiffin is equipped with a myriad of safety features to ensure a worry-free user experience. From temperature sensors that regulate the heat to prevent overheating, to a secure and robust rechargeable battery system, JordFins prioritizes the safety of its users. The smart tiffin is designed with user convenience in mind, incorporating features that go beyond mere warmth retention. With intelligent controls and a user-friendly interface, the tiffin offers a seamless experience, allowing users to customize their food's temperature according to their preferences. Additionally, safety mechanisms such as automatic shut-off and heat-resistant materials contribute to the overall reliability and security of the product.

About Us

MR. JAYANT KUMAR GAUTAM

Founder JordBhai Company Pvt Ltd.

Meet Mr. Jayant Kumar Gautam, a remarkable 14-year-old entrepreneur and the visionary founder of JordFins. Despite his young age, Jayant has already left an indelible mark on the world of innovation with his groundbreaking company. JordFins specializes in creating cutting-edge products that revolutionize the way we approach everyday tasks, particularly in the realm of food preservation. One of their standout offerings includes a unique line of products designed to keep your tiffin warm with just the press of a button.

Jayant's journey into entrepreneurship began as a passion project driven by his insatiable curiosity and desire to make a positive impact on people's lives. Fueled by an entrepreneurial spirit that belies his age, Jayant has demonstrated an exceptional ability to think outside the box and turn innovative ideas into reality. His dedication to creating user-friendly and practical solutions has quickly set JordFins apart in the competitive market.

As the founder of JordFins, Jayant wears many hats, from a visionary leader to a hands-on problem solver. His youthful energy, coupled with a keen sense of business acumen, propels JordFins forward into uncharted territory. In a world where age is no barrier to success, Jayant Kumar Gautam stands as a testament to the limitless possibilities that arise when passion meets determination in the exciting realm of entrepreneurship.

MR. GYAN CHAND GAUTAM

Co-Founder JordBhai Company Pvt Ltd.

Introducing Mr. Gyanchand Gautam, not only the father but also the co-founder of JordFins, the innovative company founded by his 14-year-old son, Mr. Jayant Kumar Gautam. Despite lacking any prior experience in the business realm, Mr. Gyanchand embraced the role of a co-founder with enthusiasm and determination, showcasing that a fresh perspective and a supportive attitude can triumph over conventional wisdom.

In a world where business acumen is often associated with years of experience, Mr. Gyanchand's journey challenges the norm. His leap into entrepreneurship alongside his son underscores the power of belief, resilience, and the willingness to venture into uncharted territory. As a co-founder, he actively contributes to the company's decision-making processes, blending his life experience with Jayant's youthful exuberance to create a dynamic and harmonious entrepreneurial partnership.

This father-son duo not only defies age stereotypes but also demonstrates the strength that lies in family support. Mr. Gyanchand Gautam's journey as a co-founder, despite lacking a traditional business background, adds a unique and inspiring dimension to the story of JordFins, highlighting that passion and dedication can often trump formal experience in the unpredictable world of entrepreneurship.
